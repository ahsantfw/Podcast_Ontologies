# Script Generation - What Does Client Actually Want?

## Current Output

The current script generation produces:
- Quotes with timecodes
- Basic segments
- Simple intro/outro
- Music cues

## Client Requirements (From Recording)

### What Client Said:
> "I would like to use you as a primary source and creative partner in building episodes for a new show which uses exclusively the podcast transcripts and book text as source material. The show has a runtime of 40-60 minutes and follows similar form and structure to 'The Midnight Miracle' podcast, **interweaving stories and ideas from different guests that revolve around central themes and ideas with music underneath and as a bridge of connective tissue**."

### Key Phrases:
1. **"Creative partner"** - Not just pulling quotes, but creating something new
2. **"Interweaving stories and ideas"** - Not just listing quotes
3. **"Music underneath and as a bridge of connective tissue"** - Music is integral
4. **"Revolve around central themes"** - Thematic organization
5. **"The Midnight Miracle" style** - Conversational, flowing, narrative-driven

### Success Criteria:
> "Can it thoughtfully compile an accurate script for a tapestry style documentary, pulling from multiple episodes and themes and **tying the final result into a magic bow**?"

**"Magic bow"** = Elegant, flowing, well-connected narrative

---

## What "The Midnight Miracle" Style Means

Based on the description, this style is:
- **Conversational** - Flows like a conversation, not a list
- **Interweaving** - Stories and ideas blend together
- **Musical** - Music is part of the narrative, not just background
- **Thematic** - Ideas revolve around themes
- **Flowing** - Natural transitions, not mechanical

This idea—that our mental state shapes our creative output—echoes through other conversations. Phil Jackson, in a different context, talks about getting centered before games. The connection isn't explicit, but it's there: creativity requires a certain state of mind.

*[Music: Transition, building]*

**Better:**
